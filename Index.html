About Daystar Academy

Our Mission

To provide quality education to children from poor backgrounds who cannot afford school fees, helping them achieve their dreams through disciplined learning.

Our Vision

To create a generation of empowered leaders through accessible education, breaking the cycle of poverty in our community.

Our Values

Discipline, Excellence, Compassion, and Community Development.

What We Offer

Quality Curriculum

We follow the national curriculum with additional life skills training to prepare students for real-world challenges.

School Meals

Nutritious meals provided daily to ensure students can focus on learning without hunger distractions.

Community Support

We work closely with parents and local leaders to create a supportive learning environment.
