About Us

Located along the **Malaba-Busia Road at Lukolis Centre**, **Daystar Academy** is a beacon of hope for bright but financially disadvantaged students. We believe every child deserves access to **quality education**, regardless of background. Our school provides **fully free schooling**, covering tuition, books, and uniforms, ensuring no child is left behind due to poverty. With a focus on **discipline, moral values, and academic excellence**, we nurture students to become responsible leaders and achievers of their dreams. Our dedicated teachers, structured learning environment, and holistic development programs empower students to break the cycle of poverty through education. **At Daystar Academy, we don’t just teach—we transform lives.**  **Join us in supporting the future of these deserving children!**
